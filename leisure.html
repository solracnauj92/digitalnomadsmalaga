Why Malaga For Digital Nomads?

Málaga has it all, from the narrow streets of the Old Town to the modern infrastructure. So, what are you waiting for? With 65 unique and unusual things to do in Málaga (and counting), it's time to start planning your next adventure! You are not limited to creative and historical sites in Malaga; you can also wander through the mountain, explore the seas or even go shopping in local or international well-known brands! You also have concerts, theatres and clubs. The possibilities are endless! Please go ahead and see some of the options.




Scuba Diving

Scuba diving is available at more than 100 different dive sites along 240 kilometres of stunning Mediterranean coastline.

Hiking and Trekking

There are so many incredible hikes in Malaga that it's difficult to decide which ones to do. The region is home to many of the best hikes in Andalucia, ranging from peaks to ravines, rivers, and coastal hikes.

Flamenco Art

Flamenco is core to Spanish culture. It's more than just music or dancing; it's an experience that you have with all of your senses.

Arabian Spa and Bath

A traditional Arabic bath where you can enjoy various therapies, as well as a place to unwind and entertain your senses.

Coast of the Sun

Wander around Málaga's stunning coastline, or the Costa del Sol (Coast of the Sun) as it is now known, which is one of the world's most famous stretches of sea and sand.

Sailing through the sea

Enjoy an unforgettable sailing tour on the turquoise-blue sea, sailing through the island's most beautiful bays. Food is served, and if you're lucky, you might even see dolphins!
